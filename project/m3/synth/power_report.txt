STATUS: POWER ESTIMATION NOT AVAILABLE
---------------------------------------

Power estimation requires a post-PnR netlist with annotated switching
activity (SAIF) and a full placement/routing solution to compute:
  - Dynamic (switching) power: requires clock net capacitances + toggle rates
  - Static (leakage) power: requires placed cell instances + corner data
  - Internal power: requires cell-level charge/discharge waveforms

The full OpenLane flow (floorplan → place → route → OpenROAD sta/power)
was not completed in this run. Yosys direct synthesis produced a mapped
netlist (synth_mapped_netlist.v) but without placement, net capacitances
are unavailable, making vectorless power estimation unreliable.

REFERENCE — CF06 Crossbar MAC Power (OpenLane 2.3.10, same PDK):
-----------------------------------------------------------------
For context, the CF06 crossbar_mac design (768 cells, 8,130 µm²) measured:
  - Internal power    : 1.629 mW
  - Switching power   : 2.091 mW
  - Leakage power     : 10.2 µW
  - Total power       : 3.720 mW
  at nom_tt_025C_1v80, 100 MHz, default activity factor.

The M3 top design is significantly larger (estimated 4,000–6,000 mapped sky130
cells vs. 768 for crossbar_mac), so total power is expected to be in the range
of 15–25 mW at the same PVT corner and clock frequency, dominated by the Q15
datapath switching activity through q15_mac (1430 XOR gates, 698 XNOR gates
visible in the pre-map stat report).
